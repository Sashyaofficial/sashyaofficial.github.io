Connecting Ancient Worlds

Discover the timeless legacy of Anyang, Bodhisattvas, and Numismatics.

About This Project

This project explores ancient connections through history, art, and culture. By delving into Anyang's ancient city, Bodhisattvas' spiritual evolution, and the intricate designs of numismatics, we aim to bridge the ancient and modern worlds.

Topics Covered

Anyang: China’s Ancient City of Kings

Anyang is one of the cradles of Chinese civilization, home to the Shang Dynasty's capital. It holds archaeological treasures like oracle bones, early forms of writing, and bronze artifacts.

Bodhisattva Avalokiteshvara

Avalokiteshvara, the Bodhisattva of Compassion, represents the spiritual ideals of Buddhism. From India to East Asia, the figure evolved, influencing art and religious practice.

Smithsonian’s National Numismatic Collection

This collection showcases coins, banknotes, and other currency from around the world. It reflects economic history and the artistry of monetary systems.
