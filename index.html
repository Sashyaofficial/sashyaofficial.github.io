Anyang - The Cradle of Ancient China

Explore Anyang, a pivotal city of the Shang dynasty, often referred to as the cradle of ancient Chinese civilization. Renowned for its oracle bone inscriptions and burial artifacts, it provides a glimpse into early governance, religious practices, and artistic achievements through jade carvings and bronze vessels.

Cultural Insights from the Ancient East

The Ancient East holds a treasure trove of cultural, artistic, and historical legacies. Here, we explore the remarkable impact of artifacts and philosophies from this region.

Anyang - Gateway to the Shang Dynasty

The ancient city of Anyang provides a unique glimpse into the Shang dynasty. Oracle bones, bronze vessels, and jade carvings discovered here highlight advancements in governance, spirituality, and craftsmanship.

Blue and White Ceramics - The Ming Legacy

These iconic ceramics, perfected during the Ming dynasty, symbolize the peak of Chinese porcelain artistry. Their intricate designs, crafted with cobalt blue on white, became prized exports, influencing global art trends.

Corals - A Symbol of Prosperity

Corals were considered rare treasures in ancient times, often used in jewelry and sacred art. Revered for their vibrant colors, they were believed to symbolize protection and prosperity in East Asian traditions.
